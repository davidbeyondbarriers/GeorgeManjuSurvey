Why this survey matters

Your story can change lives.

When people face adversity — loss, illness, trauma, crisis — something surprising can happen. Beyond surviving, many discover an unexpected kind of growth: deeper relationships, new purpose, and a strength they never knew they had. This is post-traumatic growth.

This research, guided by George and Manju, seeks to understand those journeys. Your honest responses — whatever form they take — add to a body of knowledge that will help counsellors, researchers, and future participants find their own path forward. There are no right answers. Only your truth.
